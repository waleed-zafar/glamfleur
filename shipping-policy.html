Shipping Policy

Same-Day Delivery (LA County)

We offer same-day delivery in Los Angeles and LA County FREE of charge. If your address is in LA County, you will need to place the order by 12PM PST to receive it on the same day.

US Shipping All Orders

We offer FREE shipping via FedEx. Unless a custom delivery date is selected at checkout, all orders placed before 12 p.m. PST are shipped the same day. We are unable to ship to P.O. boxes.

Choose Your Delivery Date

If you would prefer your arrangement to be delivered on a specific date, simply use our calendar tool at checkout and choose any date you’d like within a 30-day window.

Two day and Overnight Shipping

If your preferred delivery date can't be accommodated with Standard Shipping, we offer Two-Day or Overnight Shipping for an additional charge. You'll be shown the specific shipping costs for your chosen delivery date during checkout.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPMENTS

Currently, we only service North America. However, we are actively working on expanding our reach.

PACKAGE TRACKING

We will send an email notification, including tracking information when your package has shipped.

DELIVERY INSURANCE

All orders shipped with package insurance.
