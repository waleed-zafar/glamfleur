Refund Policy

At Glam Fleur, nothing makes us happier than making you happy. That's why we strive to give you our very best in everything we do!

If you have a question or concern, please reach out to us 24/7 at support@glamfleur.com.

Product Return Terms

  • Orders can be cancelled prior to shipment for a full refund.
  • Items are eligible for return within the first 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be undamaged and in the original packaging.
  • Please provide proof of purchase directly from Glam Fleur.
  • Please note that all shipping costs are non-refundable.

Please note that products which don't meet the above terms cannot be returned.

Product Refund Terms

  • You'll receive a confirmation email once your return has been inspected.
  • Your refund will be applied to the original form of payment used at purchase.
  • Refunds may take up to 7 business days to reflect in your payment account.

If your refund hasn't been processed within the timeframe listed above, please contact us at support@glamfleur.com, so we can make things right!
